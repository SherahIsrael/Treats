Get to know us. We don't bite.



At Treats, we believe that life is sweeter with a cupcake in hand. We are passionate about crafting delicious and beautifully presented cupcakes, cakes and drinks that bring joy to your taste buds and brighten your day. Our story began with a love for baking and a vision to create a cozy and inviting space where people could indulge in delectable treats and savor moments of pure happiness. Established in 2004, Treats has become a beloved destination for cupcake enthusiasts and dessert lovers alike. What sets us apart is our commitment to using only the finest ingredients. We source the freshest, high-quality ingredients to ensure that every bite of our products are a delightful experience. From premium chocolates and farm-fresh eggs to locally sourced fruits, we take pride in the quality and taste of our cupcakes and cakes.

Our dedicated team of skilled bakers and decorators pour their creativity and expertise into each design. With meticulous attention to detail, we handcraft a wide array of flavors and designs, ranging from classic favorites to seasonal specialties. Whether you crave a rich chocolate delight or a tangy fruit sensation, we have something to satisfy every palate. At Treats, we not only aim to provide exceptional cupcakes but also create a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our cozy cafe is a haven for cupcake enthusiasts, friends, and families to come together, relax, and indulge. The aroma of freshly baked cupcakes fills the air, and our friendly staff is always ready to assist you in choosing the perfect treat. We are dedicated to delivering outstanding customer service and ensuring that your experience at Treats is nothing short of delightful. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, treating yourself, or simply enjoying a sweet moment, Treats is the perfect destination. Join us in savoring the magic of cupcakes and creating unforgettable memories, one delectable bite at a time.

Indulge. Delight. Repeat. Treats - where happiness is baked fresh daily.
